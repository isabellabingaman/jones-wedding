Ceremony

6:00 pm · The First Tee, Terravita Country Club

Location

The ceremony will be held outdoors on the green at The First Tee at Terravita Country Club and will begin promptly at 6:00 pm. The venue will be equipped with shade and fans to keep guests comfortable in the Arizona evening air.

Attire

The dress code is semi-formal with a garden party spirit. Think elegant and festive: florals, soft pastels, and anything that feels like a summer evening dressed up. Blues, greens, yellows, and purples are all very much at home here — as are bold patterns, interesting textures, and any detail that makes you feel a little bit special. A color palette and attire inspo board are below. When in doubt, lean into color and joy. For guests who plan to wear heels, heel stoppers will be available in a basket at the entrance - though chunkier heels are recommended.

Cocktail Hour

6:30 pm · Terrace Patio

Following the ceremony, guests are invited to join the newlyweds on the terrace patio for cocktail hour at 6:30 pm. Raise a glass at sunset, enjoy light passed bites, and take in the views before the evening continues inside.

Reception

7:30 pm · Terravita Country Club

The reception will move inside the Country Club at 7:30 pm, where the evening will be filled with dinner, dessert, drinks, and dancing. Guests are encouraged to stay through the last song — the evening will conclude at 11:00 pm.
